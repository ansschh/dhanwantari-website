Revolutionizing healthcare management with technology - welcome to Dhanwantari!

Dhanwantari is a cutting-edge healthcare solution aimed at modernizing the classical diagnosis facilities by reducing human contact and automating healthcare management. The project includes an android application for both patients and doctors, incorporating Telehealth, online prescription management, telemedicine and remote patient monitoring.

With a goal of improving healthcare access in rural areas, Dhanwantari has received validation from experts such as Damian Matheson and Dr. Ravi Jasuja. Join the movement towards efficient and effective healthcare with Dhanwantari.
